Request Account and Data Deletion – NPF Security Response App

This page allows authorized users of the NPF Security Response app to request deletion of their account and all associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an official email to support@safecity.com.ng with the subject line: “NPF App Account Deletion Request”.

Include the following in your message:

  • Your registered phone number
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Will Be Deleted

Once verified, we will permanently delete the following:

  • Account associated with your phone number
  • Your location history (if any)
  • Any photos, audio recordings, and activity logs collected

Data Retention Policy

If the data is related to an active case or investigation, we may retain it for up to 90 days as required by law enforcement policy before full deletion.
